The Crisis: Why India Needs Shramik
India’s blue-collar sector is a $300 billion market, yet it remains largely invisible. Millions of electricians, plumbers, and carpenters operate in an ecosystem plagued by inefficiency:
* The Intermediary Trap: Workers are heavily dependent on local agents who extract unfair commissions, leaving the worker with unstable income and zero agency.
* The Invisible Professional: Without a formal, verified digital identity, skilled workers cannot prove their expertise, making it difficult to secure high-value contracts or long-term employment.
* The Reliability Gap: For contractors and large-scale project managers, finding verified, background-checked talent is a constant bottleneck that leads to project delays and quality risks.
